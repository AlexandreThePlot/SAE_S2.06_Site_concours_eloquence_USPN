Présentation de l'équipe technique

This website dedicated to the competition was entirely made by a team of five students from the Cygnus group form the 1st year of the Computer Science BUT (BUT INFO) at the IUT of Villetaneuse, within the Sorbonne Paris Nord University.

Except for the video interview of the communication teacher Mr. Nait-Ouslimane, which was made by our classmates BALENDRAN Angela, MAZGAR Selma, BOUSSERHANE Salma, JIN Céline et JEYARAJ Jeyanika, whose exchange we filmed.

Member: Alexandre

Alexandre is a first year student in BUT Informatique and has specialised in UI design over the year. As a UI developer, he mainly worked on the realization of the visual interface of the website in HTML and CSS, with the choice of the colours, the layout of the various contents and other aspects. He also took care of the translation of the pages into English, a skill of his that has become more and more refined and thus has proved to be a real asset for the team.

Member: Mazigh

As a first year student of BUT Informatique, Mazigh, like Mahmut-Ali, has also specialised in front-end and back-end development. In addition, he also honed his writing skills, which are useful to the team. Mazigh Mohammedi, a JavaScript front-end developer and web writer, was therefore involved not only in the creation of the interactive user interface but also in writing the text content such as candidate profiles in coordination with Mahmut-Ali.

Project Manager: Varun

As a young student in his first year in BUT Informatique, Varun developed the skills necessary to take on the role of project manager and to bring the project to life, such as setting up a Discord server dedicated to the students in his group. As a project manager, UI designer and web editor, Varun Gupta coordinated each member of the team and guided them through the progress of the site in terms of choosing and evolving the interface, content and interactivity. He was also partly responsible for the interface and the different parts of the site, in agreement with the USPN.

Member: Mahmut-Ali

Mahmut-Ali is a 1st year student of BUT Informatique and specialised in front-end and back-end development following numerous projects during the year. Front-end JS developer during the conception of this website, Mahmut-Ali participated in the creation of the user interface by creating a slideshow with the images of the contest, a presentation part of the former candidates of the contest with their associated profiles, as well as the profile interface of the team that you are currently reading.

Member: Mohamed

A student in his first year of BUT Informatique, Mohamed has developed the team spirit and writing skills that have enabled him to successfully complete the tasks entrusted to him. Mohamed Lazar, Graphic Designer and Web Editor, was in charge of writing most of the textual content of the website, also bringing his experience in visual aesthetics. He also had the task of designing the poster for the eloquence contest.

A speech contest? What for?

Eloquence is not about reciting or talking, it is about the art of speech and persuasion. By voice or gesture, eloquence is essential in all sectors, wether be it in law, economy or in computer science.

Improving one's eloquence is a skill to master, one that could make us stand out from the lot, and live a fulfilling life in a personal or professional aspect. In our youth, we all dreamt of having superpowers to stand out. Eloquence is a power, one that will allow us to persuade, please, and captivate an audience by simply pronouncing words.

To do so, you have only one option: participate in a speech contest, and let your voice be heard.

Our speech contest from the 14th of April 2022

An unforgettable day for the students of the computer science department at the Sorbonne Paris Nord university, who were present for the 14th of April 2022 speech contest on the subject: metaverse, is life elsewhere ?

During this contest, 12 candidates presented their speech on said subject in front of a jury and an audience composed of students. The spectators were able to take pictures, record videos and interview each of the candidates to have their thoughts and experience.

A pleasent moment filled with emotions and a great atmosphere.

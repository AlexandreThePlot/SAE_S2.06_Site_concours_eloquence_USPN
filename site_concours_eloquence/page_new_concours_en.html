A new speech contest at the USPN

Following the success of the eloquence contest of the 14th of April organised by the communication teachers of the computer science department, the Sorbonne Paris Nord University decided to organise a new one in order to give the opportunity this time to other students of the university to participate, no matter which course they follow. Indeed, the students who attended or took part in the competition that day all very much appreciated the idea of a speech contest, which gave them a real experience. The Sorbonne Paris Nord University invites you to participate or to attend a new eloquence contest on June 17, 2022. You will find in this section several informations such as the place, the date, and the program of this day.

Program and schedule

2:15 PM - Contest preparations and seating of the public

2:45 PM - Short exchange with the winners of the previous contest

3:15 PM - Presentation of the candidates and the jury

3:30 PM - Candidates speeches

4:45 PM - Public vote and jury's decision

5:00 PM - Award ceremony

5:30 PM - Buffet

Location

The event will be hosted in the IUT amphitheater droit (right) 1, located in the Forum hall in the center of the university. You will find a map layout of the university below.
